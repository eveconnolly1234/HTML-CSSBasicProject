What class is for you?

Class Age Group What's involved
Wrigglers Under 2 years
  • Develop Fundamental motor skills
  • Music keeps activities fun
  • Equipment enhances motor skills and co-ordination
  • Cognitive Lessons keep toddlers stimulated
  • Continuous Movement means no waiting in line
Wobblers 2-3 years
  • Continuous FUN with skill-specific equipment and safe and effective exercises designed for young children
  • FUNdamental Motor Development practice
  • Non stop action designed to improve balance and co-ordination
  • Games to encourage and focus on Healthy Eating
Preschool 3-5 years
  • Continuous Fun with skill specific equipment and safe and effective exercises designed for young children.
  • FUNdamental Motor Development practise.
  • ABCs of movement! Agility Balance Coordination and Speed
  • Happy Heart Routines
  • Health & Nutrition talks and handouts
Primary 6-10 years
  • Non-stop action and fun using sport-specific equipment in a non-competitive environment
  • Participation for every child
  • Activities designed to improve motor skills, agility and coordination
  • Discussions on exercise, safety, sportsmanship and the sport of the week
